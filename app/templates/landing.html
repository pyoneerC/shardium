the idea

deadhand splits your seed into 3 pieces using shamirs secret sharing.

any 2 shards = full recovery. 1 shard alone = nothing. mathematically proven.

  • you keep shard a
  • give shard b to your beneficiary
  • shard c stays on our servers

while you're alive: recover anytime with a + b

if you die: we email you every 30 days. stop responding, shard c goes to your beneficiary. they combine b + c, recover your seed

that's it. dead man's switch for crypto.

security

  • we get hacked: we only have shard c. useless alone
  • we go rogue: same. need 2 of 3
  • deadhand shuts down: you have a + b. always recover
  • man in middle: splitting happens in your browser. seed never hits network

code is open source. go read it and ask ai.

no lock-in: we use standard BIP-39 and Shamir Secret Sharing. if deadhand disappears tomorrow, you can recover your keys using ssss-split on linux or any other standard offline tool. the emergency exit is always open.

how it works

  3. download shard a + b
  4. shard c stored encrypted on our servers

  6. no response for 90 days? shard c is released
