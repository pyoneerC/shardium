the idea

deadhand splits your seed into 3 pieces using shamirs secret sharing.

any 2 shards = full recovery. 1 shard alone = nothing. mathematically proven.

  • you keep shard a
  • give shard b to your beneficiary
  • shard c stays on our servers

while you're alive: recover anytime with shard a + b.

if you go silent: we email you every 30 days to check in. if you don't respond for 90 days, shard c is automatically released to your beneficiary. they combine b + c and recover your seed phrase permissionlessly.

that's it. dead man's switch for crypto.

not just crypto

deadhand works with any secret that uses a seed phrase or recovery key:

  • GPG keys: protect your signing identity forever
  • FIDO2 / WebAuthn: back up your hardware security keys
  • SSH keys: ensure server access doesn't die with you
  • password manager master keys: your 1Password/Bitwarden vault isn't lost

if it's a secret string that unlocks something important, deadhand can protect it.

how it works

  1. type your seed phrase (go offline first)
  2. browser splits into 3 shards
  3. download shard a + b
  4. shard c stored encrypted on our servers
  5. every 30 days we email you to check in
  6. no response for 90 days? shard c is released
  7. beneficiary recovers with two shards permissionlessly (b + c)
