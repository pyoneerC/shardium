how it works


    
this uses LSB (least significant bit) steganography. each audio sample is a number representing the sound wave at
        that moment. we modify the last bit of each sample, which is imperceptible to human hearing.


    
to hide a message:

    
    
        
  • convert the message to binary (1s and 0s)
    • 
        
  • for each bit, modify the last bit of an audio sample
    • 
        
  • the change is so small humans cant hear it
    • 
    


    
to extract:

    
    
        
  • read the last bit of each audio sample
    • 
        
  • convert the bits back to text
    • 
    


    
important: dont convert the wav to mp3 or other compressed format. compression will destroy the hidden
        data. store in google drive, dropbox, or usb. sending via whatsapp, telegram, or email will compress and
            destroy it.
