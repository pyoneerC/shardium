what happens to your crypto when you die?

you have a seed phrase somewhere. drawer, safe, whatever. you get hit by a bus tomorrow. your wife gets nothing. not because someone stole it. because nobody knows how to acces it.

seed phrases arent made for humans. theyre made for computers. 12 random words you gotta keep safe forever and somehow pass to your family. its a terrible system and everyones just pretending its fine.

i built this because it was a personal problem. then i asked around and realized everyone has this problem. nobody has a real solution.

the idea

shardium splits your seed into 3 pieces using shamirs secret sharing.

any 2 shards = full recovery. 1 shard alone = nothing. mathematicaly proven.

  • you keep shard a
  • give shard b to your beneficiary
  • shard c stays on our servers

while youre alive: recover anytime with a + b

if you die: we email you every 90 days. stop responding, shard c goes to your beneficiary. they combine b + c, recover your seed

thats it. dead mans switch for crypto.

theres nothing else like this

i researched every competitor. spent weeks. heres what exists:

  • legal wills - lawyers dont understand crypto. probate takes months
  • multisig wallets - too complex. your family wont understand
  • daos - gas fees, governance tokens, weird blockchain stuff
  • existing startups - abandoned, closed source, or want your actual keys
  • chain-specific - only work for one chain. you probably have btc AND eth

nothing does what shardium does. simple, trustless, any bip-39 seed, fully open source.

security

  • we get hacked: we only have shard c. useless alone
  • we go rogue: same. need 2 of 3
  • shardium shuts down: you have a + b. always recover
  • man in middle: splitting happens in your browser. seed never hits network

code is open source. go read it
